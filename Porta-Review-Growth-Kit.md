# Porta Productions — Review Growth Kit

Your reviews are your best salesperson. A perfect 5.0 with more volume = higher Google ranking, more trust, and more booked shoots. This kit makes asking effortless and repeatable.

## 2. When to ask (timing is everything)

The best moment is **right when they're happiest** — the instant you deliver the final media and they're excited. Don't wait.

Recommended cadence per client:
1. **At delivery** — include the ask in your delivery email/text (template below).
2. **+2 days** — if no review yet, one friendly nudge.
3. **Stop there.** Never ask a third time; it gets awkward.

Aim to ask **every** happy client. If even 1 in 3 converts, you'll add reviews fast.

## 3. Templates

### A. Delivery email add-on
Paste this at the bottom of the email where you send final files.

> **One quick favor.**
> If you loved how the listing turned out, a 30-second Google review means the world to a small studio like mine — and helps other agents find me.
> 👉 [Leave a review here](https://search.google.com/local/writereview?placeid=ChIJeYR6MOvprKIRnFMhPu4Xjwc)
> Thank you for trusting me with your listing. — Ariel

### B. Standalone text / SMS (most effective)
Short, personal, sent from your phone.

> Hey [Name]! So glad you loved the [address] media 🙌 If you have 30 seconds, would you mind leaving a quick Google review? It genuinely helps me a ton: [link]
> — Ariel, Porta Productions

### C. Follow-up nudge (+2 days, only if no review)

> Hey [Name] — no pressure at all, just floating this back up in case it got buried. A quick review would mean a lot whenever you have a sec: [link] 🙏

### D. In-person / verbal script
Say it on-site or on the delivery call:

> "If you're happy with how these come out, the single biggest thing you can do for me is a quick Google review — I'll text you the link so it's one tap."

Then **send the text before you leave** so it's done while you're top of mind.

---

## 4. Make every review pay off twice

- **Reply to every review** (takes 20 sec). It signals you're active and professional, and Google rewards engagement. Thank them by name and mention the listing.
- **Repost 5-star reviews** to your Instagram story and tag the agent — social proof + free exposure for them = they remember you next listing.
- The site already pulls your best reviews into the testimonials section. As new standouts come in, send them to me and I'll swap them onto the site.
