## Part 1 — The Content Engine

You already produce the hard part: cinematic footage on every shoot. The mistake most studios make is delivering it to the agent and never using it for *their own* marketing. Every listing you shoot is raw material for 5–7 pieces of content. Here's the system.

### The "1 Shoot → 7 Posts" rule
From a single listing, cut and post over the following ~10 days:

1. **The Listing Reel** (your hero) — 20–40s vertical, music-driven walkthrough. Tag the agent + brokerage.
2. **Twilight / golden-hour single** — your best dusk frame as a standalone photo post. These stop the scroll.
3. **Photo carousel** — 6–8 stills (exterior → kitchen → primary → backyard). "Swipe through this [city] listing."
4. **Behind-the-scenes story** — you on the A7SIII / gimbal / drone. Process content builds the *brand* (this is what makes agents want to hire you, not the agent who listed it).
5. **Detail / texture clip** — 5s slo-mo of a fixture, water feature, or architectural line. Pure craft flex.
6. **"Just Listed" graphic** — agent-tagged, repostable by them (free reach into their audience).
7. **Result post** (when it sells) — "Sold in X days" overlay on the original reel. Proof that your media moves homes.

> Set a rule: **no shoot leaves the edit bay without at least the Reel + 1 BTS clip cut for your own feed.**

### Content pillars (rotate these)
- **The Work** (60%) — listing reels, twilight, carousels. Your portfolio in motion.
- **The Craft / BTS** (25%) — gear, process, how a shoot comes together. This converts agents.
- **The Proof** (15%) — reviews, "sold in X days," agent shout-outs, brokerage logos. (Pull from your new 5.0★ reviews and the brokerage strip on the site.)

### Sample 2-week cadence
| Day | Post | Pillar |
|-----|------|--------|
| Mon | Listing reel (newest shoot) | Work |
| Tue | BTS story (gimbal/drone) | Craft |
| Wed | Twilight single | Work |
| Thu | Review screenshot + "book your shoot" | Proof |
| Fri | Photo carousel | Work |
| Sat | Detail/texture slo-mo | Craft |
| Sun | Rest or repost agent's "Just Listed" | — |
| Mon | "Sold in X days" result post | Proof |
| ... | repeat with next shoot | |

### Caption framework (hook → value → CTA)
> **Hook:** "This [city] listing didn't sit on the market."
> **Value:** "Cinematic film, twilight stills, and a vertical reel — the full package for [agent]'s seller."
> **CTA:** "Agents: DM 'LISTING' and let's make yours the one buyers can't scroll past."

Always: tag the agent + brokerage, add the city/neighborhood as text + hashtag (#DallasRealEstate #[Suburb]Homes), location-pin the property's city.

### Cadence for outreach (keep it light, do it weekly)
